### **Symptom**

On attempting to invite a new user to the RedApp, the following error is seen: 

![error\_failed\_to\_invite\_users.png](/docs-test/imported-assets/redstor/4000219761/c069d524964c.jpg)

### **Cause**

This error presents if no identity provider has been selected for the relevant company.

### **Solution**

Go to the relevant company profile and enable at least one of the three sign-on providers. You can select which identity providers a company can use for single sign-on by going to their company profile. 

  

1\. Locate the relevant company, expand its menu to the right and go to **Company profile**.

  

![company\_profile.png](/docs-test/imported-assets/redstor/4000219761/2748a0dc7862.jpg)

  

2\. Go to the **Sign-on** tab. Enable at least one identity provider for this company to use, then click **Save changes** at the top right.

  

![Screenshot\_\_816\_.png](/docs-test/imported-assets/redstor/4000219761/228f22b136d8.jpg)

  

You can now reissue the invitation to the user.
