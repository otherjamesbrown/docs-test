### Log in to SpamTitan

As part of your setup, an email was sent to the supplied email address with information to get started, including credentials for your Customer Admin account. If you are an MSP customer, this email is sent by your MSP administrator.

After signing in for the first time, you should change your password and enable two-factor authentication for your account. See [Update your Security Settings](/docs-test/titanhq/products/spamtitan/docs/skellig-9/update-your-security-settings/ "Update your Security Settings").
