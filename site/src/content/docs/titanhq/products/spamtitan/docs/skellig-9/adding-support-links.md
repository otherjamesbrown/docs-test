### Adding Support Links

Go to **Settings** > **Support** to customize the links to your support services for your customers. Once added, these links are visible to your customers in their profile ![profile-icon-small.jpg](/docs-test/imported-assets/spamtitan-skellig/59479-adding-support-links/a460165411c6.jpg) menu.

![ST-SK-customer-admin-profile-menu.jpg](/docs-test/imported-assets/spamtitan-skellig/59479-adding-support-links/e84f7838017e.jpg)

:::note
For an MSP Admin, the Support and Contact links available in your profile menu are set by the system admin.
:::

Follow the steps below to customize your support links.

1.  Go to **Settings** > **Support**.

    -   _Support Desk URL_: Enter a valid URL (default: [https://helpdesk.spamtitan.com/support/solutions/folders/4000037958](https://helpdesk.spamtitan.com/support/solutions/folders/4000037958)).

    -   _Contact_: Enter a valid email address (default: blank).

2.  Select **Save Changes**.

Once added, the Support and Contact links are visible in a customer's profile menu.
