### Reporting

SpamTitan offers several reporting options. Go to **Reporting** in the side bar menu where the following options are available:

-   _Scheduled_: Allows you to schedule daily, weekly or monthly reports that can be emailed to a single or multiple email addresses. See [Creating a Scheduled Report](/docs-test/titanhq/products/spamtitan/docs/skellig-9/creating-a-scheduled-report/ "Creating a Scheduled Report").

-   _On Demand_: Generate a report showing activity for today, the last seven days or all reporting periods. See [On Demand Reports](/docs-test/titanhq/products/spamtitan/docs/skellig-9/on-demand-reports/ "On Demand Reports").

-   _Archive_: View scheduled or on demand reports that have been archived. See [Viewing Archived Reports](/docs-test/titanhq/products/spamtitan/docs/skellig-9/viewing-archived-reports/ "Viewing Archived Reports").
