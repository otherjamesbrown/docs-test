## Managing a Single Domain

![ST-SK-smaller-image.jpg](/docs-test/imported-assets/spamtitan-skellig/55545-managing-a-single-domain/82c697555c32.jpg)

Welcome to SpamTitan! A **Domain Admin** can use the information in this guide to manage email for a single domain.

Domains are added at the Customer level, usually by a Customer Admin or an MSP Admin. See [SpamTitan Levels of Access](https://support.titanhq.com/document/preview/57497#UUID-438107cd-319d-6e0d-f594-1531f80dca4a).Levels of Access for Email Security
