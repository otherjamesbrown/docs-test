#### Archived Reports

Go to **Reporting** > **Archived Reports** to view all reports that have been archived from **Reporting** > **Todays Reports** or **Reporting** > **Scheduled Reports**.

Use the Type:, Frequency:, Domain: and From: drop-down menus to filter the display:

![ST-701-reporting-filters.jpg](/docs-test/imported-assets/spamtitan-legacy/1207-archived-reports/5dc61d91126f.jpg)

The following options are available for each report listed:

1.  _Download_: Click either Text, Excel or PDF to download that format.

2.  _Delete_: Click this icon ![ST-701-delete-button.jpg](/docs-test/imported-assets/spamtitan-legacy/1207-archived-reports/3b2dc809e314.jpg) to delete the selected report.
