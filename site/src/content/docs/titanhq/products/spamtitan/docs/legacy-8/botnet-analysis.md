##### Botnet Analysis

If botnet analysis is enabled, DNS validation is performed on the first untrusted relay looking for signs of a potential botnet infected host. For example, no reverse DNS, a hostname that would indicate an ISP client or other hosts that aren't intended to be acting as a direct mail submitter outside of their own domain.

Go to **Anti-Spam Engine** > **Settings** > **Botnet Analysis** to enable or disable botnet analysis.
