#### Domain Group

A Domain Group is a group of domains that have been grouped for the convenience of controlling access by Domain Group Administrators.

From the **Anti-Spam Engine** > **Domain Group** tab, the Domain Group Administrator can view their domain group name, set their time zone and view license details.
