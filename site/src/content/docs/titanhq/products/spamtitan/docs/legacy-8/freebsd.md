#### FreeBSD

Free Unix-like operating system descended from AT&T UNIX via BSD UNIX.  Although for legal reasons FreeBSD cannot be called UNIX, as the direct descendant of BSD UNIX, FreeBSD's internals and system APIs are UNIX-compliant.
