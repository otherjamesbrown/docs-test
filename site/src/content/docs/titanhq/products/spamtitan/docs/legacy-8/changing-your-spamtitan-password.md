#### Changing your SpamTitan Password

To change the password for the logged-in user, go to **Settings** > **User Management** > **Change Password**.

1.  Enter the current password in the _Old Password:_ field.

2.  Enter a new password in _New Password:_ field. Passwords should be at least ten characters in length and are case-sensitive.

3.  Re-enter the new password in the _Confirm New Password:_ field.

4.  Click **Change...**.

A good password should contain a mix of uppercase and lowercase letters as well as numbers and punctuation. To prevent possible [dictionary attacks](/docs-test/titanhq/products/spamtitan/docs/legacy-8/dictionary-attacks/ "Dictionary Attacks"), a password should not spell out any words found in the dictionary. Passwords are case-sensitive.
