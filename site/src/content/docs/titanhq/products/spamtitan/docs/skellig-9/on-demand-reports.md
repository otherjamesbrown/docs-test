#### On Demand Reports

Go to **Reporting** > **On Demand** to create on demand reports. On demand reports are purged daily. If you wish to maintain a report for longer, it can be archived.

##### Adding an On Demand Report

Follow the steps below to create an on demand report.

1.  Go to **Reporting** > **On Demand** and select **Add**.

    ![ST-SK-add-on-demand-report.jpg](/docs-test/imported-assets/spamtitan-skellig/61240-on-demand-reports/5771ea7b36e8.jpg)

2.  From _Timeframe of the report to the be generated_, select the reporting period. Select either Today, Yesterday, Last 7 Days or All.

3.  Select **Add**.

The report will generate and appear in **Reporting** > **On Demand** when ready (this can take a few minutes).

##### Archiving an On Demand Report

On demand reports are purged daily, but you can follow the steps below to archive an on demand report. This makes the report available in **Reporting** > **Archive**.

1.  Go to **Reporting** > **On Demand** and select the on demand report you want to archive.

    ![ST-SK-on-demand-report-archive-button.jpg](/docs-test/imported-assets/spamtitan-skellig/61240-on-demand-reports/95a6d55342a8.jpg)

2.  Select **Archive**.

3.  The report is now archived in **Reporting** > **Archive**.
