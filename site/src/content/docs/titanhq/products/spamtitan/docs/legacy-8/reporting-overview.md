### Reporting Overview

SpamTitan Cloud offers several reporting options. Go to **Reporting** where the following tabs are available:

-   **History**: a record of all mail that is processed by the system.

-   **Reports**: allows you to generate several on-demand reports.

-   **Todays Reports**: once a report has been configured and run, it will appear here.

-   **Schedule Reports**: allows you to schedule daily, weekly or monthly reports that can be emailed to a specified email address or multiple email addresses.

-   **Archived Reports**: shows all reports that have been archived in Todays Reports or Scheduled Reports.
