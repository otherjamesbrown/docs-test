#### Forgot your password?

Follow these steps to reset your password:

-   Go to the log-in page and click the **Forgot your Password?** link. The Password Assistance window displays.

-   Enter your email address and click **Send**.

-   A temporary password will be sent to your email. Log in and follow the steps to [change your password](/docs-test/titanhq/products/spamtitan/docs/legacy-8/changing-your-spamtitan-password/ "Changing your SpamTitan Password").
