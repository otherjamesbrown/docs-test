As part of your Email Security setup, an email was sent to the supplied email address with information to get started, including credentials for your Customer Admin account. If you are an MSP customer, this email is sent by your MSP administrator.

Log into Email Security at [platform.titanhq.com](https://platform.titanhq.com/auth/sign-in).

:::note
You can configure SAML SSO to use your existing Identity Provider as an authentication method for the platform. Go to Configuring SAML SSO for details. Once you have configured SAML SSO, you can [log in using SAML SSO](/docs-test/titanhq/products/phishtitan/kb/getting-started-customer-admin/log-in-using-saml-sso/ "Log in Using SAML SSO").
:::

When you log in, you will see the **Overview** page.

![PT-Customer-Dashboard.jpg](/docs-test/imported-assets/phishtitan-kb/4000201282/4f95c667c9de.jpg)

-   If you are a new Email Security Customer admin, continue with the remaining setup steps to configure your account.

-   To see information about the logged-in account and to log out, select the profile ![profile-icon.jpg](/docs-test/imported-assets/phishtitan-kb/4000201282/5225ccde9cb8.jpg) icon.
