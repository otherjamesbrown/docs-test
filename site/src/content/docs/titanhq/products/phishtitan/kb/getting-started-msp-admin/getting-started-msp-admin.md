3.  [Add a Customer Account](/docs-test/titanhq/products/phishtitan/kb/getting-started-msp-admin/add-a-customer-account/ "Add a Customer Account").

4.  [Connect and configure a Customer Account](/docs-test/titanhq/products/phishtitan/kb/getting-started-msp-admin/connect-and-configure-a-customer-account/ "Connect and Configure a Customer Account").
