#### Email Security MSP Overview

Select **Email Security** > **Overview** to see a summary of activity for all your customers and a list of your customers. The default view is the last 30 days, but you can change this by selecting **Last 7 Days** in the upper right corner.

![PT-MSP-Dashboard.jpg](/docs-test/imported-assets/phishtitan-docs/62896-email-security-msp-overview/5a5bd9200918.jpg)

-   **Emails Analyzed**

    Here you can see the total number of emails processed for the selected time period.

    :::note
    This number is based on the email count and not the number of recipients who received the email. For example, if an email has 5 recipients, the email is only counted once.
    :::

    ![PT-Emails-Analyzed-MSP.jpg](/docs-test/imported-assets/phishtitan-docs/62896-email-security-msp-overview/33948b538161.jpg)

-   **Incidents**

    You can see the total number of phishing emails received by your customers for the selected time period in the **Incidents** section. Select this box to go to **Incidents**, where you can view, resolve, and manage incidents.

    ![PT-Incidents-MSP-Dashboard.jpg](/docs-test/imported-assets/phishtitan-docs/62896-email-security-msp-overview/c3cd70e656bf.jpg)

-   **Active Users/Licenses Issued**

    In this section, you can compare the combined total of your customers' active users to the overall number of Email Security licenses that were issued by you. Select this box to go to **License Usage**, where you can monitor your relationship with your customer based on licenses and usage.

    ![PT-ActUsers-LicIssued-MSP-Dashboard.jpg](/docs-test/imported-assets/phishtitan-docs/62896-email-security-msp-overview/7073349a3a7f.jpg)

-   **Emails Analyzed vs Incidents**

    View a comparison of emails that have been processed compared to the number of incidents received.

    ![PT-EmailsAnal-vs-Incidents-MSP-Dashboard.jpg](/docs-test/imported-assets/phishtitan-docs/62896-email-security-msp-overview/6078f1df4d1a.jpg)

    -   Hover your mouse over the graph to display a summary for a particular date.

        ![PT-Overview-EmailsAnal-vs-Incidents.jpg](/docs-test/imported-assets/phishtitan-docs/62896-email-security-msp-overview/7e9a44f3ba9c.jpg)

    -   Select and de-select the labels to add and remove items from the graph.

        ![PT-Overview-Labels.jpg](/docs-test/imported-assets/phishtitan-docs/62896-email-security-msp-overview/a49df7c7314d.jpg)

-   **Email Security Customers**

    This is a list of all your customers to whom you have issued licenses.

    ![PT-all-customers-table.jpg](/docs-test/imported-assets/phishtitan-docs/62896-email-security-msp-overview/0a16a5bbebd8.jpg)

    Use the Search ![PT-Search-Box-All-Customers.jpg](/docs-test/imported-assets/phishtitan-docs/62896-email-security-msp-overview/c9c140a356cd.jpg) box to search the names of your Email Security customers.

    Select the column ![Old-Column-Icon.jpg](/docs-test/imported-assets/phishtitan-docs/62896-email-security-msp-overview/f94eedba0d6f.jpg)icon to display the Customers table column names. Slide each on and off to show or hide a column.

    -   _Customer_: Name of your customer.

    -   _Description_: A short description of your customer.

    -   _Active Users_: These are M365 active licensed users. The number is updated daily based on the active user license.

    -   _Licenses Issued_: The total number of licenses issued by you to all of your customers.

    -   _Date Created_: Month, day and year that your customer was issued licenses and added to the system.

    -   _Connection_: Indicates if a customer is connected to a Microsoft 365 account. The ![Plat-Microsoft-365-Indicator.jpg](/docs-test/imported-assets/phishtitan-docs/62896-email-security-msp-overview/9249643b6edc.jpg) label shows which customers are connected; the ![Plat-Add-Connection-Link.jpg](/docs-test/imported-assets/phishtitan-docs/62896-email-security-msp-overview/c8fb2e63e50a.jpg) link highlights those customers who are not.

        Select the **Add connection** link to begin [Connecting and Configuring a Customer Account](/docs-test/titanhq/products/phishtitan/docs/email-security/connect-and-configure-a-customer-account/ "Connect and Configure a Customer Account").

    -   _Actions_: Select the view ![view-icon-small.jpg](/docs-test/imported-assets/phishtitan-docs/62896-email-security-msp-overview/4f7d1bd09d2e.jpg) icon for a customer to drill down to the customer level to view and further manage that customer.

-   **Most Targeted Users**

    View a list of the most attacked email addresses, with the most targeted user listed first. The **\# Targeted** column indicates the number of malicious emails sent to the user.

    ![PT-Most-Targeted-Users.jpg](/docs-test/imported-assets/phishtitan-docs/62896-email-security-msp-overview/ac3d040a586c.jpg)

To add a new customer, see [Add a Customer Account](/docs-test/titanhq/platform/docs/add-a-customer-account/ "Add a Customer Account").
