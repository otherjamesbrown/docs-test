## ![Plat-PhishTitan-icon-large-on-right.jpg](/docs-test/imported-assets/phishtitan-docs/62578-email-security/0d359017e839.jpg)Email Security

Email Security (PhishTitan) is TitanHQ's next-generation [phishing protection and remediation solution](https://www.titanhq.com/phishing-protection/). It works within M365 and scans all emails, both internal and external, to block and remediate threats like business email compromise (BEC), account takeover, VIP impersonation, and zero-day attacks. Using AI (artificial intelligence), ML (machine learning), and proprietary threat intelligence, Email Security provides a highly effective solution to counter new and sophisticated threats.
