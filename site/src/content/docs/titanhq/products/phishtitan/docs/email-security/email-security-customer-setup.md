### Email Security Customer Setup

Welcome to Email Security! A customer admin who has been onboarded to Email Security by their MSP and received their login credentials can follow the information here to connect and configure their account.

![pt-divider-narrow.jpg](/docs-test/imported-assets/phishtitan-docs/62500-email-security-customer-setup/d5cd4db959f0.jpg)

Before following the steps below, answer the following:

-   _Do you use Link Lock as part of your SpamTitan service?_

    -   _Yes?_: Link Lock must be disabled in SpamTitan to allow Email Security to process mail accurately.

        See [Disable Link Lock in SpamTitan](/docs-test/titanhq/products/phishtitan/docs/email-security/disable-link-lock-in-spamtitan-65973/ "Disable Link Lock in SpamTitan").

![pt-divider-narrow.jpg](/docs-test/imported-assets/phishtitan-docs/62500-email-security-customer-setup/d5cd4db959f0.jpg)

Once you've answered the questions above, follow the steps below to connect and configure your account.

1.  [Log into Email Security](/docs-test/titanhq/products/phishtitan/docs/email-security/log-into-email-security-66146/ "Log into Email Security").

2.  [Update your Security Settings](https://support.titanhq.com/document/preview/32364#UUID-605945ae-1661-5fa8-c313-64f3707d030b).Update your Security Settings

3.  [Connect to Microsoft 365](/docs-test/titanhq/products/phishtitan/docs/email-security/connect-to-microsoft-365-62975/ "Connect to Microsoft 365").

4.  [Add a Non-Delivery Report Email Address](/docs-test/titanhq/products/phishtitan/docs/email-security/add-a-non-delivery-report-email-address-62976/ "Add a Non-Delivery Report Email Address").

5.  [Add a Journaling Rule](/docs-test/titanhq/products/phishtitan/docs/email-security/add-a-journaling-rule-62977/ "Add a Journaling Rule").

6.  [Deploy TitanHQ for Outlook](/docs-test/titanhq/products/phishtitan/docs/email-security/deploy-titanhq-for-outlook-in-microsoft-365-66781/ "Deploy TitanHQ for Outlook in Microsoft 365").
