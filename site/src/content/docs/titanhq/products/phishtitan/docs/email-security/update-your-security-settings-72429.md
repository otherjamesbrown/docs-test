#### Update your Security Settings

After signing in for the first time, change your password and enable Two-Factor Authentication (2FA) for your account.

-   See [Changing your Password](/docs-test/titanhq/products/phishtitan/docs/email-security/changing-your-password/ "Changing your Password").

    If you forget your password, see [Forgot your Password?](/docs-test/titanhq/products/phishtitan/docs/email-security/forgot-your-password/ "Forgot your Password?").

-   See [Two-Factor Authentication](/docs-test/titanhq/products/phishtitan/docs/email-security/two-factor-authentication/ "Two-Factor Authentication") to enable 2FA for your logged-in account. For an MSP admin, this does not enable 2FA for all customer accounts on the system, just your own account.
