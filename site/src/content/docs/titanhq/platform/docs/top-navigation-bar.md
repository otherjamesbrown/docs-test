## Top Navigation Bar

In the navigation bar located at the top of the page, the following items are displayed:

-   **Breadcrumbs**: Appearing as a trail of links on the left side of the menu, the breadcrumbs help you to see where you are in the Platform system. The last link on the right is the page you are currently on, and you can use the breadcrumb links to navigate back through the hierarchy.

    ![Plat-Breadcrumbs.jpg](/docs-test/imported-assets/titanhq-platform/75330-top-navigation-bar/0716f15a80b9.jpg)

    The breadcrumbs include a colored badge to indicate the current view level. You'll see a blue MSP badge at the MSP level and a purple customer badge at the customer level.

-   **Profile Settings**: Select the Profile Settings ![profile-icon.jpg](/docs-test/imported-assets/titanhq-platform/75330-top-navigation-bar/5225ccde9cb8.jpg) icon to do the following:

    -   View your admin role. If you're an MSP, see [MSP Administrators](/docs-test/titanhq/platform/docs/msp-administrators/ "MSP Administrators") for details on the role assigned to you and the related privileges and restrictions. If you're a customer admin, go to [Customer Administrators](https://support.titanhq.com/en/75801-customer-administrators.html "Customer Administrators").

    -   Manage your security settings. See [Update your Security Settings](https://support.titanhq.com/en/72430-update-your-security-settings.html "Update your Security Settings").

-   **Display Mode**: Lets you select light or dark for your display. See [Selecting Dark or Light Mode](https://support.titanhq.com/en/68155-selecting-dark-or-light-mode.html "Selecting Dark or Light Mode").

-   **Documentation**: Select the ![Plat-Doc-Icon.jpg](/docs-test/imported-assets/titanhq-platform/75330-top-navigation-bar/90bdbd3ef820.jpg)icon to go to the [documentation page](/docs-test/titanhq/platform/docs/welcome-to-the-titanhq-platform/) for help navigating the UI and carrying out tasks.
